01 Introduction

At Marome Investments & Technologies ("Marome," "we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you visit our website or submit an inquiry.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users who visit our website at marome-investments.co.za or engage with our services. Please read it carefully.

02 Information We Collect

Personal & Contact Data

Full name, email address, and project details you voluntarily submit via our inquiry or contact forms.

Technical Metadata

Browser type, device type, pages visited, and referring URLs collected automatically to improve website performance and user experience.

03 How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery: To respond to your project inquiry and provide the services you requested.

Communications: To send confirmations, updates, and follow-up information related to your engagement.

Improvement: To analyse website usage patterns and improve the performance and experience of our website.

Legal Compliance: To fulfil obligations under applicable law, including applicable South African data protection laws.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

04 Security Standards

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Inquiry submissions are transmitted over HTTPS and stored on secured servers.

  • TLS / HTTPS Encryption
  • Secured Server Storage
  • Access Controls
  • Regular Security Reviews

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

05 Data Sharing

Marome does not sell your information. Data sharing is limited to the following circumstances:

Regulatory Compliance: When required by law, legal process, or government authority.

Service Partners: Trusted technology providers (e.g., email infrastructure) who process data on our behalf under appropriate confidentiality and security obligations.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction with equivalent privacy protections.

06 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Inquiry data is retained for a reasonable period to allow follow-up and service delivery.

Upon expiry of the applicable retention period, your data is securely deleted or anonymised from our systems.

07 Your Rights

Under the South African Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and applicable data protection law, you have the following rights:

Access

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information where appropriate.

Objection

Object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@marome-investments.co.za.

08 Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyse site usage. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies; however, some parts of the site may not function correctly without them.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
